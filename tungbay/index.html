Description

Hello, welcome to tungbay @arsdorint.is-a.dev. This is a tool that converts horizontal writing (in CJKV languages) to vertical writing style. The target user is those who want to view or write traditional-style poems in Chinese characters, or others non-latin scripts, such as Chữ Nôm, Hiragana, Hangul, ... in a traditional way. But for other purposes, such as reading a newspaper or simply a normal paragraph vertically, this tool is also useful.


The website is currently in development, especially to support Latin scripts in a proper way as well. You can try experience the current functions and give me some feedbacks to my email. Thank you so much for your support!

Instructions

You can choose your desire literature type (normal text or poem), and font weight for better visualization.


For poem, there is a choice to convert your poem into "Kieu Story Style", which is most suitable for "lục bát" (six-eight) poem. The style resembles The Tale of Kieu" (1866 version)'s one, with some modifications.
