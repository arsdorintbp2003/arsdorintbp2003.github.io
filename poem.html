Introduction

Hello, welcome to my poem blog - one of my corner. This is a collection of all my poem written in Vietnamese National Script (Chữ Quốc Ngữ) from 2015 until now. I have an ambitions to translate them to Vietnamese Old National Script (Chữ Nôm) for a difference visualization and bring a traditional and nostalgia vibe to my poem.


If you have any comments on my poems, feel free to do so. It is just "a small amount of salt in the big ocean", so I don't mind receiving even bad comments about my poem.


The website is currently in development. Feel free to give me any feedbacks to my email. Thank you so much for your support!
